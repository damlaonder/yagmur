about me

I am a mechanical engineering student at Purdue University with experiences in undergraduate research within engineering education and sports engineering as well as an internship with Wilson Sportings Goods Co. as a plastics engineer. My motivations in my studies are that I am inherently curious about understanding dynamic movement, specifically with how the human body moves mechanically such as through skateboarding, rock climbing, at impacts (football tackles), and more in the sphere of biomechanics.

In conjuction with my engineering pursuits, I enjoy practicing art. This ranges from studying animation technology to practicing urban sketching. A lot of my artistic pursuits are inspired by Pixar films. More specifically, Pixar’s Soul inspired me to seek out how I could combine my interests in engineering with creative storytelling.
